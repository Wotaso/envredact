Local processing of .env content

EnvRedact runs as a static client-side tool. Text that you paste into the input field is processed by JavaScript in your browser. The application has no backend endpoint for this content and does not intentionally upload your input or redacted output to Wotaso.

Hosting access data

When you open this website, technically necessary access data may be processed by our web server or hosting provider. This can include the requested URL, date and time of access, IP address, user agent, referrer URL, transferred data volume, and status codes.

This processing is necessary to deliver the website, keep it secure, and diagnose technical issues. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Cookies, analytics, and storage

EnvRedact does not set cookies, does not use analytics, and does not store your .env content in browser storage. The copy button writes the redacted output to your clipboard only after you click it.
